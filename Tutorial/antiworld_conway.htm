For every world an antiworld exists where everything is inverted. All living cells are dead and dead cells are alive.

To create antiworld the rules are written down as a switchboard:

0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
L                  
D                  
  • L stands for living cells.
  • D marks dead cells.

The following grid shows the standard life rules where three neighbours create living cells and living cells with two or three neighbours stay alive:

Conway-Rules
0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
L                  
D                  

The antiworld rule are created by rotating the state of the switchboard by 180° (no mirroring or tilting):

Anti-Conway-Rules
0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
L                  
D
